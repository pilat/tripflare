# ADR-NNNN: Short Decision Title

- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-XXXX
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Supersedes:** ADR-XXXX (omit if none)

## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, the requirements. What made a
decision necessary. State facts and pressures, not the choice itself — a reader
should feel the tension before seeing the resolution.

## Decision

What was chosen, stated plainly. "We will ..." Present the actual mechanism in
enough detail that the consequences below make sense.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, and what it costs. Be honest about the downsides — an ADR
with no negative consequences is hiding something. Include operational effects
(what breaks on crash, what a user gives up).

## Alternatives Considered

The viable options that were rejected, and why. One short paragraph each. This is
the part future readers come back for — "did they think of X?" Yes, and here's
why not.
